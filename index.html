You have 10 Stellar.

Level: 1

HP: 1

You will earn 30 Stellar per computer hacked.

You deal 0 damage.

Stellar is the main currency. Upgrades will increase your damage and automatically 'hack' for you- each one will grant more and more damage. To level up, you must deal more damage than the enemy's health. You will not gain any Stellar if you do not deal enough damage to take out the enemy, and you can click the 'Last Level' button to go back a level. Upgrades will slowly and slowly get more expensive, but will get stronger and stronger the more you buy!
